Bay Art Connect is a website designed to connect artists and other freelancers in the Bay Area. This site was Bode's capstone project for their Hackbright Academy Software Engineering Fellowship, which they completed in July of 2019.

Bay Art Connect is built with a PostgreSQL database, a Flask-implemented Python backend, and an HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React frontend. Bode also deployed the site themself, on AWS Lightsail, served via HTTPS.
